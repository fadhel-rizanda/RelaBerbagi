Our Background

In this digital era, technology has an important role in various aspects of life, including efforts to increase solidarity and social awareness. One example is by using a website application to manage donations of goods.  A website-based goods donation application can be a platform that brings together those who want to donate goods with those who need them. This platform can help increase the efficiency and effectiveness of the aid distribution process, as well as encourage more people to participate in social activities.  Indonesia is a country with a high level of social awareness. This can be seen from the many social activities organized by various communities and organizations. However, in its implementation, social activities are often hampered by a lack of coordination and information.

Our Goals

The aim of creating this website-based goods donation application is to:

  • 1. Building a platform that brings together parties who want to donate goods with parties who need them.
  • 2. Increasing the efficiency and effectiveness of the aid distribution process.
  • 3. Make it easier to access information for those who want to donate goods and those who need it.
  • 4. Encourage more people to participate in social activities.
  • 5. Strengthening the sense of solidarity and social care in the community.

Benefits you will get

This website-based goods donation application is expected to provide benefits for various parties, including:

  • 1. Simplify the process of distributing donations.
  • 2. Obtain information about needs in the field.
  • 3. Make it easier to access information about available help.
  • 4. Get the right help that suits your needs.
  • 5. Strengthening a sense of solidarity and social care.
  • 6. Increasing community participation in social activities
